Programmers in Orbit

Programmers in Orbit (PiO's) is a team of programmers with various fields of interest, originating from the Technological Educational Institute (TEI) of Piraeus.
It's purpose is gathering active and promising students NOT ONLY from the TEI of Piraeus but from (quite literally) everywhere in order to come together in subgroups and develop open source projects of their liking.

Those projects will be available in the "Projects" section of this homepage in one of two possible formats (or even both): Online and Downloadable.

Online:
     The application is web-based and can be executed online. A link will be provided to the executable online application.

Downloadable:
     The application can be downloaded and executed on the users PC.

Specifications and possible limitations concerning compatible Operation Systems (OS) will be given in the description of the applications. Also in the description will be the download link. All applications will also come with a link to the respective github or other repository containing the source files of each project. For rights to access,modify and reuse the source code contact the respective developers. Contact and general information about the PiO's team members can be found in the "The Team" section of this homepage.
